Paper 11 — Stress-Testing the IO Framework

Subjects the framework to an extended adversarial stress test against CMB perturbations, BAO absolute distances, and BBN. It derives the Schur-complement curvature formula, identifies a species-dependent baryon correction, and introduces a modular-projection idea that substantially improves the framework's observational viability relative to its earlier form.
