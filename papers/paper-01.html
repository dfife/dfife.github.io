Paper 1 — A Black Hole Cosmology

Introduces the Interior Observer framework: the observable universe lives inside a Schwarzschild black hole and the CMB is interior Hawking radiation. The paper derives zero-free-parameter predictions for the CMB temperature, dark-energy density, MOND acceleration scale, and Hubble rate, and compares the resulting expansion history with DESI DR1 BAO data.
