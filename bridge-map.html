Status Guide

How to read the claim labels used across the bridge map.

DERIVED Numerically computed from the IO geometric stack with zero fitted parameters and directly comparable to observation.
THEOREM Formally proved algebraic or structural closure inside the framework rather than a numerical prediction.
DERIVED/SCOPED Derived inside a stated observable class, sector, or quotient; extension beyond that domain is still open.
CONDITIONAL Depends on explicitly stated premises or identifications that are not yet theorem-grade.
NO-GO A proposed mechanism or extension was formally shown to fail and is recorded with the same rigor as positive results.
